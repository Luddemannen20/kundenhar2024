Fiction

Fiction is a literary genre that consists of stories created from the author's imagination rather than based on real events. It includes various forms like novels and short stories and spans numerous genres such as fantasy, science fiction, mystery, and romance. Fiction allows writers to explore themes, develop characters, and build unique worlds, offering readers entertainment and insights into the human experience.
